## Phase 1: Beta (Concept Validation)

**Goal**: Core functionality with scripted data/accounts.
**Audience**: Focus groups & SMEs.

- [ ] **Core Functionality**: The essential value loop is working.
- [ ] **Context**: Uses scripted data or pre-provisioned accounts (not full self-serve).
- [ ] **Objective**: Validate the concept. Identify what is truly valuable vs what is noise.
- [ ] **Feedback Loop**: "Is this useful?"

---

## Phase 2: MVP (Pilot Readiness)

**Goal**: Commercially ready product that works end-to-end.
**Audience**: Pilot customers (Real life usage).

- [ ] **Production Quality**: Stable, secure, and usable.
- [ ] **Marketing**: Low profile. Direct sales/onboarding of pilots.
- [ ] **Objective**: Generate customer feedback on what works/doesn't in the real world.
- [ ] **Feedback Loop**: "What is missing? What is broken?"

---

## Phase 3: Releases (R1, R2, ...)

**Goal**: Expanding functionality and marketing activity.
**Audience**: Early Majority / Broader Market.

- [ ] **Feature Expansion**: Addressing gaps found in MVP pilot.
- [ ] **Marketing**: Active promotion and lead generation.
- [ ] **Objective**: Grow the user base and refine the feature set.

---

## Phase 4: V1.0 (Market Fit)

**Goal**: Sustainable adoption and customer-driven direction.
**Audience**: General Market.

- [ ] **Signal**: Customer adoption rate meets expectations.
- [ ] **Dynamics**: Feedback loops are defining the roadmap.
- [ ] **Objective**: Optimization and Scale.
