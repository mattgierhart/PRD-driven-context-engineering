# Risk Discovery Question Bank

## Opening Questions

Use these to frame the interview:

- "Let's stress-test your plan. I'll ask challenging questionsâ€”the goal is to find risks we can mitigate, not to kill the idea."
- "Imagine it's 6 months from now and the product failed. What's the most likely reason?"
- "What's the thing you're most worried about that you haven't told anyone?"

---

## Market Risk Questions

**Competition:**
- "What happens if [competitor] launches this feature in the next 60 days?"
- "What would a well-funded competitor do to block you?"
- "Why hasn't [incumbent] already built this?"

**Demand:**
- "What evidence do you have that users will pay for this?"
- "How confident are you in your market size estimate?"
- "What would cause the market to shrink?"

**Timing:**
- "Is there a market window closing? When?"
- "What macro trend could invalidate your thesis?"
- "Are you too early or too late? How do you know?"

---

## Technical Risk Questions

**Complexity:**
- "Which feature has the most technical uncertainty?"
- "What have you never built before?"
- "What could take 10x longer than you expect?"

**Dependencies:**
- "What external API or service could break your product?"
- "What happens if [critical dependency] changes its API?"
- "What integration is most complex?"

**Performance:**
- "What happens at 10x your expected load?"
- "Where could latency become a problem?"
- "What data growth could break your architecture?"

**Security:**
- "What data are you storing that would be bad if leaked?"
- "What compliance requirements apply?"
- "What would a security researcher find in your code?"

---

## Adoption Risk Questions

**Onboarding:**
- "What's the biggest friction point in [UJ-001]?"
- "How long does it take to reach the 'aha moment'?"
- "What could cause a user to give up during setup?"

**Behavior Change:**
- "What behavior change are you asking users to make?"
- "What habit must users break to adopt this?"
- "How long until users see value?"

**Retention:**
- "What would cause a user to churn in week 1?"
- "What competitor feature could pull users away?"
- "What's your plan if activation rate is 50% of target?"

---

## Resource Risk Questions

**Scope:**
- "If you had to cut scope by 50%, what stays?"
- "What's the minimum viable version of this?"
- "Which feature would you cut if it took 3x longer than expected?"

**Team:**
- "What skill does the team lack?"
- "What happens if a key team member leaves?"
- "Do you have the right expertise for [complex feature]?"

**Budget:**
- "How long is your runway for validation?"
- "What's your burn rate during development?"
- "What happens if you can't raise the next round?"

---

## Dependency Risk Questions

**External Services:**
- "What external service could block your launch?"
- "What's your fallback if [critical API] is unavailable?"
- "What happens if [vendor] changes pricing by 10x?"

**Partners:**
- "What partner relationship is critical?"
- "What happens if [partner] says no?"
- "Who else must cooperate for this to work?"

**Regulatory:**
- "What regulatory requirement could block launch?"
- "What compliance certifications do you need?"
- "What legal review is required?"

---

## Timing Risk Questions

**Deadlines:**
- "Is there a hard deadline? What happens if you miss it?"
- "What external event affects your timeline?"
- "What's driving the launch date?"

**Seasonality:**
- "Is there seasonality in your market?"
- "When is the worst time to launch?"
- "What market event could accelerate or delay you?"

---

## Follow-Up Questions

Use these to dig deeper:

- "What makes you say that?"
- "What would change your mind?"
- "What evidence would prove you wrong?"
- "Who disagrees with you on this?"
- "What's the worst-case scenario?"
- "How would you know if this risk is materializing?"
